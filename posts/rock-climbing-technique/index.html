Specific climbing technique videos

Heel hook

I was getting cramps and trigger points in my hamstrings and glutes for harder heel hooks. Make sure to work on strenghening and streching them. Warm up before the session with some deadlifts/one leg deadlifts, hamstring streches and drills on the wall where possible.

  • Mercedes Pollmeier - How to Heel Hook For Climbing | Instructional Video https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zIY4AzlXMtI

  • Catalyst Climbing - TOP 4 TIPs for Beginner heel hooks - Louis Parkinson https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eDmYLqBegCw

Toe hook

This was a game changer for me for toe hooks, but also getting a shoe with good toe rubber

  • Catalyst Climbing - Toe Hook Tips - you didn’t know this was a thing https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=QQgchljSkF4
